League of Legends, commonly referred to as League,
is a 2009 multiplayer online battle arena video game
developed and published by Riot Games.
Inspired by Defense of the Ancients, a custom map for Warcraft III,
Riot's founders sought to develop a stand-alone game in the same genre.
